Frequency Map

The frequency map tracks how many times each element appears in our stack.

The frequency map keeps track of how many times each element appears in our stack. In this case:

  • Element 5 appears 2 times
  • Element 7 appears 2 times
  • Element 4 appears 1 time

Group Map

The group map organizes elements by their frequencies, grouping elements that appear the same number of times.

Group Map: {1:[4], 2:[5,7]}

Frequency: 1
Elements: [4]
Frequency: 2
Elements: [5, 7]

Frequency Map: {5:2, 7:2, 4:1}

Element: 5
Frequency: 2
Element: 7
Frequency: 2
Element: 4
Frequency: 1

The group map organizes elements by their frequency:

  • Frequency 1 contains: [4]
  • Frequency 2 contains: [5, 7]

This allows us to quickly find all elements with a specific frequency.

Maximum Frequency Tracking

We also keep track of the maximum frequency (maxfreq) to quickly find the most frequent elements.

Maximum Frequency: maxfreq = 2

This tells us that the most frequent elements appear 2 times in our stack.

Group Map: {1:[4], 2:[5,7]}

Frequency: 1
Elements: [4]
Frequency: 2
Elements: [5, 7]

By keeping track of the maximum frequency (maxfreq = 2), we can quickly access the most frequent elements through the group map.

When we need to find the most frequent elements, we simply look up the elements stored at the maxfreq key in our group map.

These three data structures work together to provide efficient operations:

  • The Stack stores all our elements in order
  • The Frequency Map tracks how many times each element appears
  • The Group Map organizes elements by their frequencies
  • The Max Frequency provides quick access to the most frequent elements

This organization allows for optimized approach to various operations like retrieving most frequent elements.
